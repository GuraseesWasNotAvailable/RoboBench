Zero-shot WidowX, four open models

Four open robot policies, one WidowX arm, no task-specific fine-tuning. Seven Bridge-style jobs, 100 starts each: 2,800 closed-loop attempts. Octo leads the average. SpatialVLA leads the spatial tasks. OpenVLA and RT-1-X are strong on drawers. Sink is 0 for every model.

Overall scores are close. Task profiles are not.

Each model gets 700 identical evaluation episodes. Bars show overall success rate (finishes / attempts) with a Wilson 95% range. Overlapping ranges mean a tie on the average.

Octo leads by a small margin. Octo: 244. 700 (34.9%). SpatialVLA: 231. 700 (33.0%). The gap is small and the Wilson ranges nearly overlap. OpenVLA is at 19.4% and RT-1-X at 15.1%. The mean alone misses that they win different tasks.

Task difficulty varies a lot

Average success across the four models, by task. Close drawer is easy. Sink is 0 under this protocol.

Difficulty is uneven. Close drawer averages 67.8%. Sink averages 0%. Stack is near zero for everyone except SpatialVLA. A single "Bridge score" mixes an easy drawer task with a suite failure.
